Receptionist AI

How It Works

From ringing phone to booked appointment, in four steps

1

The call comes in

The AI receptionist answers instantly, every time — no hold music, no voicemail, no "we'll call you back." It's branded and trained to sound like your business, not a generic bot.

2

It has a real conversation

Instead of a rigid phone tree, it understands what the caller actually needs — a quote, a question, an existing job update — and responds naturally, adapting as the conversation moves.

3

It qualifies and captures

Name, number, reason for the call, urgency, timeline — all captured automatically, with low-quality or spam calls filtered out before they ever reach your team.

4

It books and hands off

Consultations get scheduled directly on your calendar, the lead lands in your CRM, and urgent requests get flagged for immediate follow-up — all before you've picked up a phone.

In Practice

It's 9pm on a Saturday and a homeowner calls with an urgent issue. The AI receptionist answers, recognizes the urgency in what they're describing, captures the details, and flags it as high-priority in your system — instead of going to voicemail and getting a call to a competitor who did answer.
